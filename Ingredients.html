Product Care & Quality

01. How to Enjoy
  • Shake Well: Since we use 100% fresh fruit and natural palm sugar, ingredients may settle. Shake to blend the flavors perfectly.
  • Serve Chilled: Best enjoyed ice-cold for maximum refreshment.
  • Get Creative: Drink straight from the bottle or use as a high-protein topping for your morning granola.
02. Storage
  • Keep Refrigerated: Store between 2°C and 5°C. Limit time at room temperature to under 2 hours.
  • Avoid Sunlight: Heat can harm live probiotics and alter the flavor profile.
  • Shelf-life: Best within 7 days of production. Consume within 24 hours once opened.
03. Safety Note

If the bottle appears heavily bloated or has an unusually sour aroma before the expiry date, do not consume it. Please contact our team immediately for a replacement.
